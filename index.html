I. Introduction

  • My name is Yaming Mao, if you can do me a favor, just call me Eugene.

    Born in China, I stayed there for 18 years. After my 18-year-old birthday, I went to America for my college education.

  • I have kept doing artworks since I still stayed in my mother's womb.

    I am so fantastic that my parents fear that I will be greater than Nicolas Cage. Hence, they prevented me from applying NYU Tisch and sent me to UGA.

  • However, times have passed and circumstances have changed. Justin Bieber, replacing Nicolas Cage, becomes the best.

    I feel really really bad. It is urgent to realize that we are losing the real artists just like Nicolas Cage, so I decide to come forward, fighting for the art.

  • Everything is hard in the beginning.

    I need to sell myself in order to get commission. That is why I did this website. This website is my first step to introduce myself to the world, to let people know what I am doing, and to become a real artist. I put three important projects I've done below and attach a paragraph of description to my artwork. I also give my contacts at the buttom of the website. If you want to give me a job or buy my artworks, please don't hesitate to message me!

  • Enjoy yourself!
